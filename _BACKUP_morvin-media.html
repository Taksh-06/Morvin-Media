Common Questions

Everything
You Need to Know.

How long does a typical project take?
+
Timelines vary by scope. A product shoot can be turned around in 3–5 days. A full brand film typically takes 2–4 weeks from briefing to final delivery. We always agree on timelines upfront and stick to them.
Do you work with small businesses or startups?
+
Absolutely. Some of our best work has been with ambitious early-stage brands. Premium production isn't just for big companies — it's how small brands compete with the big ones. We tailor our packages to suit different budgets without compromising on quality.
What cities do you operate in?
+
Our studio is based in Surat, Gujarat. We regularly shoot across Gujarat and Maharashtra, and we're available for national projects. Travel and logistics can be arranged for the right project — get in touch to discuss.
What do I need to prepare before a shoot?
+
Very little — that's the point. We handle all pre-production including scripting, storyboarding, mood boards, and location scouting. You bring your products/talent and trust the process. We brief you thoroughly so nothing is left to chance on the day.
Can I see examples of your work before committing?
+
Of course. We're proud of our portfolio. During your initial consultation we'll walk you through case studies and showreel content relevant to your industry. We believe the work speaks for itself.
How do we get started?
+
Fill in our contact form below, send us a WhatsApp, or email us directly. We'll book a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs and share how we can help. There's no pressure — just a conversation.
